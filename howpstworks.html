PST Community offers a structured yet flexible learning environment for various stages of programming.

There are three learning tracks: Beginner, Junior, and Senior, each covering topics suitable for different experience levels.

Weekly sessions include lectures, hands-on coding exercises, and interactive discussions to help members progress systematically.

Experienced mentors lead sessions, provide guidance, answer questions, and offer personalized feedback.

Members have access to tutorials, coding challenges, past session recordings, and recommended reading materials.

Members can join live sessions or access recorded content at their convenience, accommodating different schedules and learning paces.
